About

Hello! I'm Laura.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio - here you will find my projects, thoughts and reflections on my software testing journey.

I've been using my time in Covid-19 lockdown to finally make time for my passion - tech, and get stuck into learning about software testing.

I come from a design background, earning a degree in Clothing Design and Technology in 2013. Since then, I have discovered a keen interest in web development and design, and most recently, software testing. I am using my spare time to work through courses, get familiar with programming languages and practice my testing skills by contributing to crowdtesting projects.

Thank you for taking the time to view my work, if you would like to contact me please get in touch.
