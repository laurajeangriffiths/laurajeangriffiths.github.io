Testing is the process of improving a software product or feature by seeking and addressing risks. Identifying possible risks at all stages icreases the likelihood of the software functioning as intended. Identifying, and testing for risks at an early stage is beneficial to the SD process as it saves time on time-consuming reworking later down the line. Software intended for business that contains issues could be problematic to the businesses reputation as well revenue. Predicting and investigating not only things that are likely to, or are problematic, but also to think outside the box and search deeper.
